A modern, interactive word search puzzle generator powered by AI. Create custom word search puzzles with intelligent word placement, multiple difficulty levels, and export to PDF.

## Features

- **AI-Powered Word Placement**: Uses advanced algorithms to intelligently place words in the grid.
- **Customizable Grid Size**: Choose from various grid sizes to fit your needs.
- **Multiple Difficulty Levels**: Adjust the difficulty of the puzzle with different word lengths and placements.
- **Export to PDF**: Save your word search puzzle as a PDF for easy printing and sharing.
- **Interactive Interface**: User-friendly interface for creating and solving puzzles.
